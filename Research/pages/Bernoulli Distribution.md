In [[Probability]],

- 2 possible outcomes,
- 1 trial

$$
P(X=x) = P^x (1-P)^{1-x}
$$

where $X$ is the number of heads and $x \in \{0, ~1\}$.

## Bernoulli Trials

- Independent. $P(A_1 \cap A_2 \cap A_3 \cap \cdots \cap A_n) = \prod\limits_{k=1}^n P(A_k)$
- Stationary. Same $P$.
