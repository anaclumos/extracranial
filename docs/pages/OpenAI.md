OpenAI is an [AI](./../.././docs/pages/AI.md) research and deployment company that aims to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity.
AGI is a [AI](./../.././docs/pages/AI.md) type that can outperform humans at the most economically valuable work.
OpenAI consists of a non-profit corporation and a for-profit subsidiary.
OpenAI also provides an [API](./../.././docs/pages/API.md) for accessing new [AI](./../.././docs/pages/AI.md) models they developed.
