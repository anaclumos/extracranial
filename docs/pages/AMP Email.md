> [AMP](./../.././docs/pages/AMP.md) for [email](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md) allows senders to include [AMP](./../.././docs/pages/AMP.md) components inside rich, engaging [emails](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md), making modern app functionality available within the [email](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md). The [AMP](./../.././docs/pages/AMP.md) [email](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md) format provides a subset of [AMP](./../.././docs/pages/AMP.md) [HTML](./../.././docs/pages/HTML.md) components for use in [email](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md) messages, allowing recipients of [AMP](./../.././docs/pages/AMP.md) [emails](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md) to interact dynamically with content directly in the message. [AMP Email](https://amp.dev/about/email)
