Bondee is a virtual avatar social app created by [Singapore](./../.././docs/pages/Singapore.md)-based tech firm [Metadream](./../.././docs/pages/Metadream.md). Users can chat and interact with each other on this social media platform via its [metaverse](./../.././docs/pages/Metaverse.md) environment. In addition, users can also create and customize their [3D](./../.././docs/pages/3D.md) characters and design rooms resembling their dreams. Bondee was launched on January 7, 2023, and has topped multiple [app store](./../.././docs/pages/App%20Store.md) charts across Asia.
