How are [AI](./../.././docs/pages/AI.md) and [ML](./../.././docs/pages/ML.md) different? [AI](./../.././docs/pages/AI.md) is the general terminology of machine mimicking human intelligence. [ML](./../.././docs/pages/ML.md) is considered a subset of [AI](./../.././docs/pages/AI.md). [ML](./../.././docs/pages/ML.md) is the process of developing intelligence.
