> The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) is the revenue service for the [United States](./../.././docs/pages/United%20States%20of%20America.md) federal government, which is responsible for collecting [U.S.](./../.././docs/pages/United%20States%20of%20America.md) federal [taxes](./../.././docs/pages/Tax.md) and administering the Internal Revenue Code, the main body of the federal statutory [tax](./../.././docs/pages/Tax.md) law. It is part of the Department of the Treasury and led by the Commissioner of Internal Revenue, who is appointed to a five-year term by the President of the [United States](./../.././docs/pages/United%20States%20of%20America.md). The duties of the IRS include providing [tax](./../.././docs/pages/Tax.md) assistance to taxpayers; pursuing and resolving instances of erroneous or fraudulent [tax](./../.././docs/pages/Tax.md) filings; and overseeing various benefits programs, including the Affordable Care Act.
