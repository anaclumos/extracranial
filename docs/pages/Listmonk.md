Listmonk is a free and open-source [email](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md) marketing tool that allows you to send [newsletters](./../.././docs/pages/Newsletter.md), promotional [emails](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md), and transactional [emails](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md). It's a self-hosted [newsletter](./../.././docs/pages/Newsletter.md) and mailing list manager that's fast, feature-rich, and packed into a single binary.

Listmonk has these features:

- Supports powerful and dynamic [email](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md) templates
- Allows you to create and manage massive mailing lists, subscribers, and campaigns
- Enables you to create customized campaigns and manage templates and media that can be reused in templates
- Uses a PostgreSQL (â©¾ 12) [database](./../.././docs/pages/Database.md) as its data store
- The [back-end](./../.././docs/pages/Back-end.md) is written in Go, and the [front-end](./../.././docs/pages/Front-end.md) is Vue with Buefy for [UI](./../.././docs/pages/UI%20and%20UX.md)
- Licensed under AGPLv3

You don't have to pay for subscriptions or worry about hidden fees with Listmonk. You only pay for hosting the service.
