<div lang='en-US'>

"Sign in with [Apple](./../.././docs/pages/Apple.md)" is a privacy-focused login feature provided by [Apple](./../.././docs/pages/Apple.md), allowing users to securely sign in to apps and websites using their [Apple](./../.././docs/pages/Apple.md) ID, with the option to share minimal personal information and the ability to hide their [email](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md) addresses.
