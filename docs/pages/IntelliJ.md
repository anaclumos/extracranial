> IntelliJ IDEA is an integrated development environment (IDE) written in [Java](./../.././docs/pages/Java.md) for developing computer software written in [Java](./../.././docs/pages/Java.md), Kotlin, Groovy, and other [JVM](./../.././docs/pages/JVM.md)-based languages. It is produced by [JetBrains](./../.././docs/pages/Jetbrains.md) (formerly known as IntelliJ) and is available as an [Apache](./../.././docs/pages/Apache.md) 2 Licensed community edition and in a proprietary commercial edition. Both can be used for commercial development. [IntelliJ IDEA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IntelliJ_IDEA)
