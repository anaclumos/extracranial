Adobe Premiere Pro CC is a timeline-based [video editing application](./../.././docs/pages/Video%20Editor.md) from Adobe Systems. It's used for editing videos, commercials, and other film, television, and online video. Premiere Pro CC also includes audio editing functionality. Premiere Pro CC works on both Windows and [Apple](./../.././docs/pages/Apple.md) computers. It's comparable to [Apple](./../.././docs/pages/Apple.md)'s [Final Cut Pro](./../.././docs/pages/Final%20Cut%20Pro.md) [video editing program](./../.././docs/pages/Video%20Editor.md). Many video producers choose Premiere Pro CC as their program of choice.
