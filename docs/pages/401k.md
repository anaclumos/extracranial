A 401(k) is a retirement plan where you put money aside until you're 59. This money is [tax](./../.././docs/pages/Tax.md)-free today, but you pay [taxes](./../.././docs/pages/Tax.md) when you withdraw it. Some employers match your contributions, which is essentially free money.
