Quota Labs specializes in B2B Equity management [SaaS](./../.././docs/pages/Software%20as%20a%20Service.md) tools for Asia.
Think as [Carta: Equity Management Solutions](https://carta.com/) for Asia.
Quota Labs has two divisions.
The [VC](./../.././docs/pages/Venture%20Capital.md) side tool and the Startup side tool.
[VC](./../.././docs/pages/Venture%20Capital.md) investment procedures are old and antiquated.
It is done through handwriting, fax, and [email](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md).
An [American](./../.././docs/pages/American.md) company, Carta, already dominates the [U.S.](./../.././docs/pages/United%20States%20of%20America.md) market.
But unlike regulated [stock](./../.././docs/pages/Stock.md) markets, Private Equity regulations vastly differ across markets.
Considering that, Carta cannot ever penetrate the [Korean](./../.././docs/pages/%EB%8C%80%ED%95%9C%EB%AF%BC%EA%B5%AD.md) market.
The essential advantage is speed.
Competitors in this [VC](./../.././docs/pages/Venture%20Capital.md) scene are slow.
While we have a small engineering team, we are full of the best in their type.
The critical point is to hold trust among businesses.
That is the key to B2B software.
Current BM revolves around subscription fees, and because VCs are using our tool, the financial stability remains strong.
Some customers require [on-premise](./../.././docs/pages/On-Premise.md) hardware, requiring us to adapt to such demands.
