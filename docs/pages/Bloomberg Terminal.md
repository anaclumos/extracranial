- [OpenBB-finance/OpenBBTerminal: Investment Research for Everyone, Anywhere.](https://github.com/OpenBB-finance/OpenBBTerminal)

The [Bloomberg](./../.././docs/pages/Bloomberg.md) Terminal, or [Bloomberg](./../.././docs/pages/Bloomberg.md) Professional, is a computer software system that provides financial professionals with real-time financial data, news, and analytics. It is a subscription-based service provided by [Bloomberg](./../.././docs/pages/Bloomberg.md) L.P., used by financial institutions, investment firms, corporations, and government agencies.

The [Bloomberg](./../.././docs/pages/Bloomberg.md) Terminal offers various [features](./../.././docs/pages/Feature.md), including financial market data, news, and analysis on multiple assets such as [stocks](./../.././docs/pages/Stock.md), bonds, currencies, and commodities. It also provides tools for portfolio management, risk analysis, trading, and research. The system also has an instant messaging service called [Bloomberg](./../.././docs/pages/Bloomberg.md) Chat that allows users to communicate with each other and share information.

The [Bloomberg](./../.././docs/pages/Bloomberg.md) Terminal is widely considered an essential tool for professionals in the financial industry, as it provides up-to-date and accurate information critical for making informed investment decisions. A [Bloomberg](./../.././docs/pages/Bloomberg.md) Terminal subscription costs are high and can be a significant expense for individual users or small firms. However, for larger institutions and investment firms, the [Bloomberg](./../.././docs/pages/Bloomberg.md) Terminal is indispensable for staying on top of the markets and making informed investment decisions.
