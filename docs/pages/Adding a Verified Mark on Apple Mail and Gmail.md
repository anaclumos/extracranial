## Adding on iPhones

Export the identity and private key at the same time. [AirDrop](./../.././docs/pages/AirDrop.md) it to your phone, go to settings and install it. Go to [Apple](./../.././docs/pages/Apple.md) [Mail](./../.././docs/pages/Mail.md) settings and turn on Sign by Default.
