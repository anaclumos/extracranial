Harry Stebbings is a British venture capitalist who started "The Twenty Minute [VC](./../.././docs/pages/Venture%20Capital.md)" podcast and launched his [venture capital](./../.././docs/pages/Venture%20Capital.md) fund in 2020. He started The Twenty Minute [VC](./../.././docs/pages/Venture%20Capital.md) podcast with $50 and not a single contact in the [venture capital](./../.././docs/pages/Venture%20Capital.md) industry. The first episode was an interview with Guy Kawasaki, released on 10 January 2015.
